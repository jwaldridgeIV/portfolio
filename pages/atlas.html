A Historical Atlas

This project was concieved as supplementary material for people reading or listening to a book or podcast on the French Revolution. My original plan for the maps in this atlas involved data that either does not exist, or cannot be easily located. This atlas has a mix of qualitative and quantitative maps, I hope you enjoy!

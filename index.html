Nice to meet you! I am Jessie, a geographer and urban historian.

I am currently an undergrad student at CU Denver, honing in technical and research skills in many disciplines. I aim to combine the holistic research of a historian with the spatial thinking of an urban planner, with a goal of making our cities into a canvas of our past, present, and future.

Creating maps is my favorite way to combine creative ideas and technical prowess. I have many interests outside of my academic ones which support my geographic pursits, like golf, linguistics, and coding, all of which give me unique insights into the world in which we live.
